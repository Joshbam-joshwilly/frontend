More About Us

Miss Nacre Africa is a social enterprise that works on achieving sustainable development by fostering gender equality, education, and youth empowerment. We are determined to uplift livelihoods and advocate for leadership inclusivity.

Our Mission

The FemmeForce program aims to encourage young women's empowerment with much emphasis on leadership participation and decision-making to solve community problems with local solutions for development. An empowered woman, a transformed community. We believe that women take vital steps to change the communities/societies starting from our homes, but rarely you find them representing or taking the lead in public areas
