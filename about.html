About Us

Miss Nacre Africa is the regional beauty pageant that aims to empower young African women with major focus in leadership, entrepreneurship and philanthropy; it is a designed platform for visionary young women to exploit an opportunity that opens up doors of positive influence and broad impact to their lives, community, country, Africa and the world at large. The story begins with Frida Uwingeneye; the founder of Miss Nacre Africa in the competition which she attended that gave platform young women in career that had projects and business ideas to enhance their growth and sustainable development of communities back in 2019.
